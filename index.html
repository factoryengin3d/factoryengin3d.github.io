TransPort Factory

The business simulation game.


You are the manager of a distribution center.
As the manager, you are in charge of sending as many goods as you can. The future of the company is in your hands.

free web templates

Your objective

As the distribution center manager your objective is to send as many goods as you can, to fulfill your task you sould hire more workers and buy more shlefs, but keep in mind you workers have their salary, the items you buy are not free and your income only depends on the quantity of products you send.

Workers

There are 2 types of workers:

  1. Blue Worker:  This worker is in charge of bringing goods and store them on the shelfs.
  2. Yellow Worker:  This worker is responsible for collecting a good from the shelf and take it to the corresponding van.

A workers' energy is determined by salary, when a worker loses all of his energy he will take a break.

You can hire new workers using the 2 buttons with the worker image in the bottom left corner of the screen.

A worker can complain if his work enviroment is incomplete, for example if there are no boxes in the warehouse or if there are no shelfs.

You start with one worker of each, As the manager you can hire more Blue and Yellow workers to increase the distribution center production.

Items

There are diferent types of items that you can buy

Shelfs

You can buy more shelfs, the number of shelfs determine the amount of goods you can store on your werehoue.
Shelfs are bought from the button with a shelf image in the left corner of the screen and you will need to place them in the warehouse.

Boxes

You can buy more boxes that need to be sent. It will be paid for each box you send. But beware, if you have boxes at the end of the day, you will have to pay a fine.
